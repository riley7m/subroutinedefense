At 1 QC = 50 Fragments:
- 500,000 Fragments = 10,000 QC = **$99.99** (whale pack)

### **2. Lab Slots Too Cheap?**
- All 3 lab slots = 21,000 QC
- Via whale pack ($99.99) = 18,000 QC (**not enough!**)
- Need $100-$200 of QC to unlock all lab slots
- **Seems reasonable for a permanent upgrade**

### **3. Lab Rush Economics**
- 1 hour = 25 QC = ~$0.25 (via whale pack)
- 24 hours = 600 QC = ~$6.00
- **Very expensive for time skip!** May discourage usage

### **4. Free QC is VERY Generous**
- Free players can earn 1.77M QC over 2+ years
- This is **97x** more than the whale pack (18K QC)
- **May reduce incentive to buy QC packs**
- Consider: Are achievements too generous?

### **Option A: Reduce Free QC**
- Cut achievement rewards by 50-75%
- Total free QC: ~500K instead of 1.77M
- Still generous, but creates more incentive to buy

### **Option B: Increase Fragment Costs**
- If fragments are too cheap, increase fragment costs 2-5x
- Makes QC‚ÜíFragment conversion more valuable
- Encourages QC purchases

### **Option C: Add More QC Sinks**
- Create exclusive QC-only items
- Seasonal events requiring QC
- Cosmetics, skins, special data disks

### **Option D: Keep Current Balance (Generous F2P)**
- Accept that free players get tons of QC
- Monetize via battle pass ($5-13/tier)
- Rely on convenience (lab rush, fragments) for whales
